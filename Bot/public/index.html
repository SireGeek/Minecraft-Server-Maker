TikTok Discord Bot Dashboard

Setup & Instructions

Welcome to the TikTok Discord Bot dashboard! This bot connects to a TikTok live stream chat and forwards messages into Discord channels by channel name.

Steps to get started:

  1. Fill in your discordToken, tiktokUsername, discordGuildId, and discordChannels in the Settings tab.
  2. Make sure the Discord bot is invited to your server with proper permissions (reading messages, sending messages, managing messages).
  3. Click Start Bot in the Bot Control tab to connect.
  4. View the bot status and logs in the Bot Status & Logs tab.

Moderation: The bot automatically deletes messages containing banned words (set in the bannedWords array in Settings).

Channel selection: Use the exact channel names in discordChannels. The bot finds channels by name automatically.

If you have any questions, check your console or the logs here!
